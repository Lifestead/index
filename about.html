About

Hi, I'm Chris.

I delayed starting this project for many years. The short answer is I was terrified. The real reason is more complicated.

I've loved writing since I was young. I'd write poems, short stories, and reflections on life. This came naturally to me. It was a way to clear my mind and gain a deeper understanding of my thoughts.

At some point, when I became an 'adult,' my writing went from a form of self-discovery to self-consciousness. I stopped following my heart. I wrote less for myself and more for the algorithm, fearing if I didn't, my writing would go unseen.

I felt the pressure to jump on trends, stand out, and monetize.

It pushed me away from the craft.

I'd love nothing more than to impact as many lives as possible through my writing, but not by losing myself again.

So, at the risk of sounding ungrateful for your valuable time, this project is for me, not you.

It's a way to show up for my 10-year-old self, after giving up on him long ago.

Maybe you've felt a similar pull to reconnect with yourself. If so, I'd encourage you to explore it.

In the meantime, I hope you find some value while you're here.

Thanks for stopping by.

— Chris
